W3DGE press kit

About the game

W3DGE is a fast-paced, minimalist arcade game about swallowing wedges before they can stab into you. The walls are closing in and the wedges are lined up endlessly in every direction. How long can you survive?

Key features

  • Fast-paced, twitch-heavy, wedge-based action
  • 5 different difficulty levels, each with their own unique challenges
  • Hypnotic, minimalist aesthetic
  • Competitive leaderboards
  • Unrelenting, energetic electronic soundtrack
